Detailed introduction

Welcome to this 4 bedroom, 2 bathroom home with an unauthorized 2 bedroom, 1 bathroom additional accommodation. The main floor has a lg living room w/ a den, open kitchen, 2 bedrooms, 4 piece bathroom and a spacious laundry room. The downstairs suite has 2 bedrooms, kitchen, 4-piece bathroom & a den off the living room. The home also has a newer gas furnace. The house offers a sunny, south facing deck. The zoning (light industrial I1) allows for many different uses such as a mini storage, veterinary clinic, & much more! The property is flat making the entire piece of land usable & perfect for RV parking. Located in Central Nanaimo, w/ access to Beban park, aquatic centre, Nanaimo Golf Club, hiking & biking trails, beaches, restaurants, cafes and shopping as well as easy commutes via seaplanes, & B.C Ferries with daily flights & sailings to the lower mainland, & surrounding islands.
